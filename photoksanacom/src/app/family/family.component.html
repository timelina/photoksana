Preparation for the family photo shooting

Preparation for the family photo shooting is very important. The shots are expected to be full of love and harmony of your family. They must give energy and positive emotions. That is why it is very important to plan family photo session in details. Below you will find my simple advise when you plan your photoshooting.

  • Choose location. If you are going to photograph your family at home, make sure there are no many furniture items in the room and there is enough space to place all family members. About locations you may also read in this article.
  • Choose outfit. I have several recommendations to the outfits in this article. In the family photography it is very important that all family membersâ€™ outfits go well together. Group colors and hues. Prepare all you are going to put on: clean and iron if possible.
  • Choose time. Choose the ideal time for your family. It depends on your family rhythm. Choose time when your kid is active and cheerful.
  • If you are with a kid, bring toys, water and snacks (not candy and chocolate) for your kid. During cold season bring a blanket with you to warm your child between shots.
  • Pay attention to your hair, nails and makeup
  • Trust your photographer and try to not pay attention to camera.
  • And finally, bring your peace and high spirit. Remember: your smiles on the photographs must be natural. Feel free to give hugs and kisses to your child. Great captures happen when all family members are busy with something: playing and having fun.
